About

I am an Assistant Researcher at LASIGE, within the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Lisbon.

With a PhD in Computer Science from Université Paris 8, I investigate how interactive technologies can be designed to foster more inclusive digital experiences. My work focuses on developing and evaluating tools that promote equitable participation across diverse user groups, in close collaboration with users to ensure solutions are grounded in real-world accessibility needs.

My research has been published in leading venues such as CHI, ASSETS, and TACCESS. I regularly contribute to the community as a reviewer and program committee member for conferences like ASSETS and CHI.
