About

I am a researcher in Human-Computer Interaction and Digital Accessibility, currently based at LASIGE, University of Lisbon. With a PhD in Computer Science from Université Paris 8, my work examines how technologies can be shaped to reduce exclusion and promote equity. I focus on the design and evaluation of accessible digital systems, drawing on empirical research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and international projects. My research engages critically with societal challenges, aiming to support inclusive practices and inform policy through actionable evidence.

Research

My work explores how digital systems can be designed to support inclusion in practice, with particular attention to accessibility, inclusive design, and the implications of emerging technologies. I focus on real-world applications examining their impact on users and their alignment with accessibility standards. Through collaborative, often cross-sectoral research, I aim to generate insights that contribute to more equitable digital practices in education and public services.
